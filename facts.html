Essential Facts about the Rising


104 years ago, six days, five nights. Take a close look at some well-known and obscure facts of the Rising. How many of these facts did you know?



Air-brushed out of history? An interesting insight from Elizabeth O’Farrell

#Irishwomen

Pearse was standing in his greatcoat, with O’Farrell mostly obscured, only her feet and skirt can be seen. Since O’Farrell’s feet were deleted from some versions of the iconic photo, it is often suggested that women were literally "written out" of Irish history.

The forgotten heroine

#Irishwomen

Elizabeth O’Farrell died at the age of 75 while on holiday in Fatima House in Bray, Co. Wicklow and she is buried in the Glasnevin Cemetery next to Julia Grenan in the republican plot.

The Brave Seven

#Irishmen

Easter Rising was solely planned by 7 men of the Military Council. In the image, Sean Mac Diarmada, who planned the Rising initially with other 6, Patrick Pearse was one of them.

No Survivors

#Seven Leaders

The seven members of Irish Republican Brotherhood Military Council who planned the Rising, all were executed after the Rising. Most of the executions took place over a ten-day period.
