Past and Present Gallery



They say a picture reveals more than a thousand words. Moore Street has changed a lot throughout the years. More precisely, it has changed a lot after the 1916 Easter Rising. Learn about Moore Street visually and see for yourself how much this oftentimes neglected street in central Dublin has changed.

About us
We are a group of students at Trinity College Dublin. Creating this website was a project for a module of our Master's course Interactive Digital Media.
Our objective
Our objective
This website serves to inform about Moore Street in a historic context, focusing on the 1916 Rising. We are in no way affiliated with any political views regarding Moore Street and its history.
